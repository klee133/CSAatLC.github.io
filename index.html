Who are we?

Comprising Fordham College Lincoln Center undergraduate students, the Commuting Students Association is a branch of student government that works closely with the Administration to accommodate commuter students and to ensure representation and comfortability on campus. We offer programming tailored to the needs and concerns of commuting students in order to help foster involvement in the Fordham community.

Events

We plan to provide virtual events for our commuter students, as well as prepared to transition into hybrid and normal in-person events.

Game Night

CSA’s Game Nights have garnered an infamous reputation for intense gameplay amongst students.

It’s a time where commuters can destress and play games with other students, giving them that informal space to de-stress and relax with others after a long day of school and other commitments.

Coffee Break

CSA sponsors three Coffee Breaks every semester, which provides commuters with a break from academic life and a chance to connect with other commuters.

We have good food and drinks, so that you can hang out and relax with your friends!

CSAW

Every semester, we have one week of events with the purpose of celebrating our community-- the commuters!

We have giveaways, we have dinner outings, we have recreational activities, we have movie nights, you name it! Join us in the fun!
